Part 2: Enact

Now proceed through Questions 1, 2, and 3 and respond to the question prompts. As you read through each question, you should be looking at that same corresponding Layer on your student cards.

Navigation Instructions: When you click the arrow at the bottom of the screen, you will proceed to the first question of the enact section of the activity. On the left hand side of the screen, you will see tabs cooresponding to each student that you drew from the deck. When you click on those tabs, you will be able to see your student card and navigate between their layers. Each new layer will unlock for the cooresponding question. You will be able to edit to edit your responses for that layer.
On the top of the screen, you will see your subject card and any responses to previous questions. You will be able to view them but will not able to edit them once they have been completed.
